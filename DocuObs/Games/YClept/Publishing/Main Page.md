**Yclept** – a grim adventure where a Кing is destroying his own city, and you must stop him. To survive, you must navigate the game board, hunt down and eliminate his accomplices, and frame others to draw the king's wrath away from yourself.

Each night, someone is guaranteed to die. Do your best to ensure it isn't you.

1. The game unfolds simultaneously in both 3D and 2D realms. Gather resources and interact with inhabitants in the 2D world to influence the events in the 3D one.

2. Your character is weak and heavily reliant on the people around you. You'll have to adapt when the Кing kills the most useful citizens at the most inopportune time.

3. You will have to sacrifice others to stay alive. There is no other way.
